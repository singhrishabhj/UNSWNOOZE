Update the UNSNWOOZE application to improve the Face Verification wake-up task by implementing a **liveliness check using head rotation**.

The alarm must only turn off after detecting a real human face and verifying that the user has rotated their head from left to right.

---

FACE DETECTION

Use a browser-based face detection library such as:

MediaPipe FaceMesh
or
TensorFlow.js face-landmarks-detection

Open the device camera and detect a face in real time.

Requirements:

* Start camera when the face verification task begins.
* Detect if a human face is present in the frame.
* Display a message if no face is detected:

"No face detected. Please look at the camera."

---

LIVELINESS CHECK USING HEAD ROTATION

Once a face is detected, the system must verify that the user rotates their head.

Instructions displayed to the user:

"Rotate your head left and then right to stop the alarm."

Implementation logic:

1. Track the **nose landmark position** or head orientation angle.
2. Record the starting position.
3. Detect when the head turns left.
4. Detect when the head turns right.

Conditions for success:

* Head rotation must pass both directions.
* Movement must exceed a small threshold angle to avoid false detection.

Example logic:

Start position detected
↓
Head turns left (angle threshold reached)
↓
Head turns right (angle threshold reached)
↓
Liveliness verified

If both movements are detected, the verification succeeds.

---

ALARM STOP CONDITION

The alarm must stop only when:

Face detected
AND
Head rotated left
AND
Head rotated right

If the movement is not completed, the alarm continues.

---

USER INTERFACE

While verification is active show:

Camera preview
Instruction text

Example:

"Rotate your head left and right to stop the alarm."

Show a progress indicator such as:

Step 1: Face detected
Step 2: Turn head left
Step 3: Turn head right

Mark each step as completed.

---

FAILURE HANDLING

If no face is detected or head movement is not detected within 10 seconds:

Show message:

"Face verification failed. Please try again."

Restart the detection process.

---

EXPECTED RESULT

When the alarm rings and the Face Verification task is selected:

1. Camera opens.
2. Face is detected.
3. User rotates head left and right.
4. Liveliness is verified.
5. Alarm stops only after successful verification.
